This is my personal HR-FAQ site for any recruitment process purposes.
Well, if you are here, one of the two following things could have happened to you:

You have googled something and suddenly landed here

Then you probably were searching for general job interview HR questions. Although this page was not made for you, i have collected a lot of questions which you can find all directly in the FAQ section. Tadam!
But before you inspire yourself with my answers, bear in mind that i am probably still unemployed. Better even not read them!

You have my application on your desk for a vacant position

In this case you are on the right place.
But i absolutely hate to talk about myself in ways you expect it. But since your questions look always the same, i decided to wrote my answers down once for all times and get rid of frustrating talks.
I have gathered here my opinions regarding most typical "HR topics" possible. It is also well suitable for pseudo-technical interviews where i would have to talk only about my experience, where there are not really technical questions.

Why this way?

Convenience

You can start the virtual HR interview part wherever and whenever you want

Response

Possibly all are included here, even some which you would maybe not dare to ask

Accuracy

There is no negative energy (because invading uninvited) in front of my screen which could influence my response

Time saving

Not need to setup any meeting for a such interview

Appreciation

Your manager will be thankful for saving his time

Peace

No possibly unplesent interactions today

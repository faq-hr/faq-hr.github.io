DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
as Test and Validation Engineer:

  • Create the testing schedule and plan the tests order
  • Lead the product validation projects in laboratory
  • Define and optimize validation plans by selection of relevant requirements and test methods
  • Prepare and execute the tests in compliance with customer requirements/international norms
  • Create and verify the test protocols and test reports
  • Report the tests results and status of assigned projects
  • Development, introduction and modification of test methods according to customer requirements
  • Identify new customer/specification requirements or product characteristics in order to anticipate readiness of suitable equipment, methods and competencies in the labs
  • Facilitate failures root cause analysis and work out on problem solving solutions
  • Supervision over technical documentation, test records and control-measuring equipment
  • Purchase of necessary tools, goods and services, liaise with suppliers and conduction of supplier evaluation
  • Arrange and coordinate testing in external labs if needed and review of received external test reports
  • Knowledge of and compliance with requirements of ISO/IEC 17025
  • Maintenance and improvement of management system
  • Timely performance of entrusted tasks in accordance with the quality system adopted at the company
